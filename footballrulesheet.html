Rule Sheet

Rules

  1. Every match is for duration of 4+4 minutes.
  2. 5+2 players with rolling substitutes.
  3. 1 break halftime interval
  4. Penalty shootout from midline in case of draw at the end of 8th minute.
  5. Extra time of 2+2 minutes is applicable only from the semi-finals.
  6. Kick-ins instead of throw-ins.
  7. Yellow card suspends a player for a minute and the player can join only from the middle of the pitch once a minute has elapsed.
  8. Severe challenges could result in the player being sent off for the match and the severity of injury could lead the referees to suspend the player for the further matches as well.
  9. Arguments with referee's decision leads to disqualification of team
  10. College ID card is mandatory.
